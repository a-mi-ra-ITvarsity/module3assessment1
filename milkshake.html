Strawberry Milkshake

Prep Time: 10 mins

Yeild: 4

Ingredients




Steps

    1. Blend half of the strawberries, 1/4 tsp of vanilla, 1 cup of ice cream and 2 Tbsp of milk in blender until smooth
    2. Pour into 2 glasses then repeat the process with remaining ingredients
    3. (Optional) Drizzle inside ofthe glass with syrup brfore pouring in the milkshake, top with sliced strawberries and serve immediately
