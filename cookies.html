Pecan, Raisin and Cinnamon Cookies

Prep Time: 10 mins

Yeild: : 5 ½ dozen

Ingredients










Steps

    1. Cream butter in a large mixing bowl; gradually add sugar, beating until light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add coffee, stirring well.
    2. Combine flour, soda, cinnamon, and allspice in a large mixing bowl. Stir in raisins and pecans to coat well; add to creamed mixture.
    3. Drop dough by heaping teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart onto greased cookie sheets. Feel free toadchocolate-chips or diced nuts on top before placing in the oven
    4. Bake at 350° for 8 minutes once baked remove from cookie sheets, and cool on wire racks.
    5. (Optional) Once cooled drizzle with melted chocolate and moremnuts
